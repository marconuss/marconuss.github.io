About

For my first project at Massive Miniteam I had the opportunity to work on Chicken Police: Paint it Red!, developed by The Wild Gentlemen and published by Handy Games. My job was to bring the noir world of Sonny Featherland to the 9th generation consoles: PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series S/X.
Alongside the porting tasks, I was also responsible for the implementation of the new haptic feedback system for the DualSense controller, which was a great challenge and a lot of fun to work on. At the time the system was fairly new, and it was a great opportunity to learn and experiment with it.

The game was developed using Unity and C#
