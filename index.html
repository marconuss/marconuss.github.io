About me

Welcome to my Portfolio!
I’m a dedicated game developer with a double Master’s degree in Digital Games from the Cologne Game Lab and the Cnam ENJMIN of Angoulême, specializing in game programming.
Currently, I’m a software engineer at Massive Miniteam in Cologne, where I’ve gained hands-on experience in console porting for PlayStation and Xbox on a range of published titles.

My passion for making videogames lies in merging technology with art to make immersive and magical experiences. But I’m also curious about exploring other engineering and creative fields, from web and app development to animation and design.
Originally from Milan, Italy, I moved to Germany for my studies, to learn games programming, but also to connect with diverse creative minds, and find new perspectives.

If you are interested in what I do, feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn!
