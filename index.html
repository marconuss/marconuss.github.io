Aether

Aether is a two player local co-op game, where each player gets to play as one of the four elements: re, water, earth or air. The players are divided by the shadow world and connected by the string, that forces them to not go too far apart. The players should work in harmony so that they don’t overstretch the string or pull each other in the wrong direction. While doing so they should also keep the music going, by pressing the button at the right time, collecting aether. The goal of the game is to keep the string till the end, avoiding shadows.

Tomorrow never knows

It’s just a normal Sunday in 1967 and Leonard doesn’t have to go anywhere. But wasn’t it Sunday just yesterday? Doesn’t every day just feel the same, his wife telling him all the same things she already said before, radio playing the same songs… Something is not right. Is it the same day over and over again? Will tomorrow ever come? You have to figure out how that happened to time and what’s going on but your memory space is very limited, so you need to choose wisely which memories you want to keep with you the next day.

Soggy

The Devil rules upon the world where his curse transforms people, who do immoral actions, into bugs. Soggy experienced this transformation on his own hand, after stealing at the market as a child. His father fell into this corruption long ago and now fights as a general on the side of evil. So begins your journey as Soggy on a quest through the nine rings of the Devil’s realm to defeat him Interact with the people, train your skills and collect weapons and armory to defeat the generals that control the districts. Watch out for the choices you make during the game. It affects the gameplay and your transformation at the end: more human or insect!?
