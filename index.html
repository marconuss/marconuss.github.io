About me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Marco Cristoforetti, a passionate game developer currently pursuing my Digital Games Master's degree at the Cologne Game Lab and working as a porting developer at Massive Miniteam.
I grew up in Milan, Italy and moved to Germany for my studies, to learn programming, but also to meet new people and find new perpectives.
My love for games is driven by my desire to unite technology and great art to make immersive storytelling experiences.
Please feel free to explore my portfolio and see my latest projects. Thank you for stopping by!
