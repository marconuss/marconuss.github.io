About

Mixed Reality pinball game combining digital elements with real world objects. Using a transparent monitor to overlay the digital world over the real 3d printed objects. Combines the familiar mechanical elements of real pinball, like the flippers and the plunger, with effects and features only possible in a virtual pinball, such particle effects and animations.

My Responsibility

Together with Kevin Walter I drafted the original concept of the idea. I was then in charge of the electronics and sensors and the communication with the engine. Additionally, the building of the box and head tracking.

Software

Godot, C#, Arduino, Visual Studio, FaceTrackNoIR
