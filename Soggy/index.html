A game by Marco Cristoforetti

About

The Devil rules upon the world where his curse transforms people, who do immoral actions, into bugs. Soggy experienced this transformation on his own hand, after stealing at the market as a child. His father fell into this corruption long ago and now fights as a general on the side of evil. So begins your journey as Soggy on a quest through the nine rings of the Devil’s realm. Interact with the people, train your skills and collect weapons and armory to defeat the generals that control the districts. Watch out for the choices you make during the game. It affects the gameplay and your transformation at the end: more human or insect!?

Software

Unity, C#, Visual Studio, Photoshop
